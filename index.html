The Bunny Buddies are a collection of 8888 hand-drawn bunnies living their best life on the Ethereum Blockchain. Designed by Ryan Robinson, these NFTs with crazy aesthetics and strong personalities are on their way to take over the metaverse.

The Bunny Buddies are a collection of unique collectible art pieces designed by Ryan Robinson aka Rhabbitz from the group of artists Yobritish. With this collection, the artist who collaborated with major labels such as Universal Music, Columbia Records or Atlantic Records explores duality and looks for the right balance between darkness and light. “This art represents my brand. On the surface you see cuteness but I might add darker elements like in rap music when you dive into it and see the further context”, says Robinson. You might have seen his work, now it’s your first chance to own it.
